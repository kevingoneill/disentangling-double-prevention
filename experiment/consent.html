Welcome to Our Study!    

Key Information: This consent form asks you to take part in a research study. The study is being conducted by Felipe De Brigard, PhD., associate professor at the Department of Philosophy, the Center for Cognitive Neuroscience, and the Duke Institute for Brain Sciences.

Purpose: This research study seeks to help us understand how humans make causal judgments.

What you will be asked to do: This research study consists of one session. You will be asked to read one or more scenarios that follow a series of events in a fictional story. Then you will be asked to answer some questions about the story. You will also be asked for some demographic information. The total time for participating in this research study is expected to take 2.5 minutes. At the end of the study you will be paid a prorated $12 per hour (the total of which is made available on this study’s sign up page). Participation in this study is voluntary, and you can stop at any time without penalty, but you will only be compensated for your time if you get to the end of the study and submit your results. To withdraw from the study, close your browser window.

How your confidentiality will be maintained: If you choose to participate, your name will not be linked to your responses at any time, and there is no link between this consent form and your responses. All data will be securely saved at all times. All data we keep will be de-identified, indefinitely.

Future Use of Data: Your de-identified data (data without your name), together with de-identified datafrom other participants in the experiment, might be made publicly available, will be shared with other researchers as a requirement of publication and funding, and might be used for future research purposes.

Benefits and Risks: This research will not benefit you personally. The researcher will be happy to discuss the details of the study with you in detail at its completion. We know of no risks to you for participating in this study.

If you have any questions about this research, please contact the IMC Lab at imagination-study@duke.edu. For questions about your rights in this study, contact the Duke IRB at campusirb@duke.edu and reference protocol #2023-0055

Clicking Accept: By clicking on the “I consent” button, you indicate that you are 18 years of age or older, that you voluntarily agree to participate in this study and that you have read the information in this consent form.
